About Us

The Magic Noodle brings authentic Chinese flavors to Las Vegas. Our hand-pulled noodles, rich broths, and fresh ingredients deliver a dining experience you’ll never forget.

Our cozy, casual dining room is a favorite for both locals and visitors. We proudly offer vegan and vegetarian options so everyone can enjoy a bowl of comfort.
